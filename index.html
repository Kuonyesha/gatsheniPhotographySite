What we do

We provide photography and video services to a wide range of clients in South Africa. Together with the clients, we design a package that best meets their needs. One client's package may not be the same as another client's package. Healthy partnerships, in our opinion, start at the individual level when we talk about affordable payment plans and accessible spending plans that work for everyone. We then choose the ideal package based on this to ensure that the client's memories are preserved in style. We have the client in mind in each and every decision we make. We shoot in broadcast quality and deliver it to par.

Skills

experience

  • Cinematography
    Prior to pressing the record buttons on our cameras, we assist the customer in creating the scene they have in mind, taking care of the lighting, and adding all the finishing touches.
  • Editing
    In situations where the client lacks the necessary experience, we explain the available techniques and standards to assist create the final product. We edit and deliver in accordance with the client's specifications.
  • Photography
    We shoot and produce high-quality images that are appropriate for use on all publishing platforms.

We have worked with corporations, government agencies, and individual clients for more than ten years recording live events, podcasts, weddings, music concerts, and interviews.
